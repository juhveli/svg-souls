# Lore Items: Archaeology of the Shatter

## I. Consumables (Ephemeral Time)

### Vial of Liquid Seconds
*   **Effect:** Restores Health.
*   **Description:**
    > *A small glass vial containing a golden, viscous fluid that twitches on its own.*
    > *"The rich drank time like wine, believing it would keep their glass skins from growing dull. They did not know that hoarding time leaves none for the rest of the world. It tastes of copper and regret."*

### Shard of Panic
*   **Effect:** Temporarily increases Movement Speed.
*   **Description:**
    > *A jagged splinter of red crystal that vibrates violently in the hand.*
    > *"Adrenaline crystallized into a solid state. Use it to borrow speed from your future self. Just remember that the debt must eventually be paid."*

### Bottled Silence
*   **Effect:** Reduces Enemy Aggro Range / Invisibility to Sound-based enemies.
*   **Description:**
    > *A heavy lead jar that seems to weigh nothing at all. Opening it releases a total absence of sound.*
    > *"Harvested from the deepest vaults of the Hushed Halls. For a moment, you do not exist, for existence is a vibration, and you have ceased to hum."*

## II. Key Items (Progression & Secrets)

### Oxidized Whistle
*   **Location:** World 1 (The Scrapyard) - Area 2.
*   **Use:** Triggers the *Rust-Dragon* when blown in the Smog Vent.
*   **Description:**
    > *A dog whistle made of rusted iron. It makes no sound when blown, but the rust on nearby metal begins to flake off.*
    > *"The scavengers used these to call the beast, feeding it scrap to keep it from eating them. It answers only to the song of decay."*

### Mannequin's Glass Eye
*   **Location:** World 1 (The Scrapyard) - The Mannequin Graves.
*   **Use:** Passive Lore Item / Tradeable?
*   **Description:**
    > *A perfectly spherical glass eye, weeping a constant stream of oil.*
    > *"The prototypes were discarded because they could not stop crying. The Architects deemed 'sorrow' a flaw in the manufacturing process, rather than a reasonable reaction to existence."*

### Perfect Prism
*   **Location:** World 2 (Glass Gardens) - Area 1.
*   **Use:** Triggers *Narcissus* when dropped in the Reflection Pools.
*   **Description:**
    > *A geometric impossibility. A prism that reflects light that isn't there.*
    > *"Vitria demanded perfection. When this prism was cut, it was so flawless that it became jealous of its own reflection. It yearns to be broken."*

### The Stopped Watch
*   **Location:** World 3 (Clockwork Arteries) - The Piston Chamber.
*   **Use:** Lore Item.
*   **Description:**
    > *A heavy brass pocket watch with hands fused to the face at 11:59.*
    > *"A keepsake of the Chief Engineer. It is said he stared at this watch for so long he forgot to wind the heavy heart of the world. Or perhaps, he chose not to."*

### The Gag of the Saint
*   **Location:** World 4 (Hushed Halls) - Velvet Sanctum.
*   **Use:** Lore Item.
*   **Description:**
    > *A strip of cloth woven from pure void. It absorbs all light around it.*
    > *"Used to silence the First Prophet who tried to warn the world about the Conductor. The monks sew their own mouths shut in imitation, terrified that speaking the truth will summon the Static."*

## III. The Keys to the Paradox (Secret Boss Trophies)
*Collecting all four allows access to the True Ending in World 5.*

### Scale of Decay (World 1)
*   **Source:** Drop from *The Rust-Dragon*.
*   **Description:**
    > *A massive, flaking scale that smells of wet iron. It is warm to the touch.*
    > *"Entropy is not a mistake; it is the universe breathing out. This scale proves that even the end can be beautiful."*

### Shard of Narcissus (World 2)
*   **Source:** Drop from *Narcissus, The Shattered Idol*.
*   **Description:**
    > *A mirror shard that shows the viewer's face, but older and wiser.*
    > *"Vanity freezes time. To love oneself is to refuse to change. To hold this shard is to accept that you are not who you were yesterday."*

### Spring of Eternity (World 3)
*   **Source:** Drop from *The Chrono-Wraith*.
*   **Description:**
    > *A mainspring that never unwinds. It holds infinite tension.*
    > *"The machine wanted to run forever. It didn't understand that a song that never ends is just noise. This spring represents the courage to stop."*

### Velvet Throat (World 4)
*   **Source:** Drop from *The Banshee*.
*   **Description:**
    > *A fleshy, preserved vocal cord wrapped in silk. It hums a low, sad note.*
    > *"The voice of the void. It does not scream; it whispers the truth that the Conductor tried to hide: The song was always meant to end."*
