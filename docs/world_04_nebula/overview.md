# World 4: The Fractal Nebula
**Theme:** Zero-G, Geometry, Light, Abstraction.
**Core Concept:** The laws of physics break down. Matter is just light trapped in a prism.

## Areas

### Area 1: The Asteroid Belt
*   **Visuals:** Floating islands of crystal in a purple void.
*   **Mechanics:** **Gravity Jumping**. You can jump between planetoids.
*   **Lore:** The debris of worlds that failed to form.

### Area 2: The Prism Fields
*   **Visuals:** Lasers refracting endlessly.
*   **Mechanics:** **Light Puzzles**. You must align mirrors to create bridges.
*   **Lore:** This is where the Grand Architect designed the "Physics" of the universe.

### Area 3: The Gravity Well
*   **Visuals:** A black hole horizon.
*   **Mechanics:** **Time Dilation**. Closer to the center = Slower time. Further away = Faster time.
*   **Lore:** The drain at the bottom of the universe.

### Area 4: The Event Horizon Edge
*   **Visuals:** A thin line of light separating existence from non-existence.
*   **Boss Arena:** A platform made of hard light.

## Bosses

### Sub-Boss: The Constellation Knight
*   **Lore:** A warrior made of stars connecting the dots.
*   **Mechanic:** Teleports between star-points.

### End Boss: The Black Hole Sentinel
*   **Lore:** Guardian of the gate to the Source. It has no face, only an event horizon.
*   **Mechanic:** It absorbs your attacks (Range). You must use melee (Gravity) to hurt it.
*   **Reward:** *Map to World 5 (The Source)*.

### Secret Boss: The Non-Euclidean Prism (Cthulhu Mythos)
*   **Gimmick Trigger:** You must solve a puzzle in Area 2 using an "Impossible Shape" item found in World 1 (Backtracking).
*   **Lore:** A shape that cannot exist in 3D space. Looking at it causes damage (Sanity mechanic). It is an intruder from the "Angles of Time."
*   **Reward:** *Fragment of the First Song (4/5)*.
