# World 5: The Event Horizon (The Source)
**Theme:** The Beginning, Pure Sound, White Space.
**Core Concept:** The place where the First Song was sung. It is a "White Room" where reality is written.

## Areas

### Area 1: The Memory Hall
*   **Visuals:** Statues of the Player from previous deaths.
*   **Mechanics:** **Review**. You fight enemies from previous worlds (Boss Rush mode).
*   **Lore:** The Architect remembers your failures.

### Area 2: The Forge of Seconds
*   **Visuals:** A giant anvil striking sparks that become stars.
*   **Mechanics:** **Rhythm Platforming**. You must move exactly on the beat of creation.
*   **Lore:** Here, time is hammered into shape.

### Area 3: The Architect's Study
*   **Visuals:** Mountains of blueprints.
*   **Mechanics:** **Puzzle**. You must fix the "Blueprints" of the world to proceed.
*   **Lore:** He tried so many times to get it right.

### Area 4: The First Sound
*   **Visuals:** A single, vibrating string in the void.
*   **Boss Arena:** The Singularity.

## Bosses

### Main Boss: The Grand Architect (True Form)
*   **Lore:** The creator of the universe. He is tired. He wants to stop the song because he knows what is coming (The Void).
*   **Mechanic:** He wields "Creation" and "Destruction." He can delete platforms or spawn new ones.
*   **The Choice:** Kill him (Ending A) or Save him (Ending B).

### Secret Boss: The Shadow of the Architect
*   **Gimmick Trigger:** Triggered if you try to leave the boss room without making a choice.
*   **Lore:** The Architect's doubt, given form.
*   **Reward:** *Fragment of the First Song (5/5)*.

### FINAL SECRET BOSS: Azathoth, The Blind Singer (The Void)
*   **Trigger:** You must have all 5 Fragments of the First Song.
*   **Appearance:** A mass of nuclear chaos at the center of infinity.
*   **Lore:** The Architect didn't create the Song; he stole it from Azathoth. The Blind Singer wants its music back.
*   **Ending:** (Ending D).
